RAMAN

About The Project

The rapid development of humanoid robots brings about new shifts in the boundaries of robotics as a scientific and technological discipline. In the context of humanoid and robotics, our aim is to build a social interactive humanoid which can communicate with other human beings like a normal person and do all the tasks a human can with better precision. We do this with the integration of various amazing technologies like computational neuroscience, Artificial Intelligence, speech recognition, compliant grasping and manipulation, cognitive robotics, robotic navigation, and perception. Project Raman is a powerful android robot being designed in the form of an adult human with the following abilities and features:

  • Detect and locate objects, faces and recognize faces.
  • Retrieve chatbot responses based on classification and clustering algorithms for pre-programmed questions and Artificial Intelligence will answer general questions.
  • Path planning, obstacle avoidance, and understanding the surrounding environment.
  • Hands that are capable of performing some physical tasks.
  • 6-wheel base for the locomotion.
  • 3D Object Localization and Tracking as well as Trajectory estimation.
